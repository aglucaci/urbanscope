Usage Notes

Use clear space. Leave at least the height of the `U` around the mark.
Scale proportionally. Do not stretch, crop, rotate, or stack the lockup into a different arrangement.
Preserve contrast. Avoid noisy or low-contrast placements that reduce legibility.
Keep the name stable. Use UMDB and Urban Microbiome Database, not UrbanScope.

Boilerplate

Short copy suitable for slides, articles, program blurbs, or repository descriptions.

UMDB (Urban Microbiome Database) is a public-facing scientific resource for discovering, organizing, and exploring urban microbiome and related environmental sequencing metadata from public repositories. The project emphasizes searchable BioProject-level discovery, run-level provenance, browser-based analytics, and reusable public release artifacts for downstream analysis, comparison, and study assembly.
